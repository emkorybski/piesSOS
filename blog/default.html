Could ozone be effective against coronavirus?

Inspired by this Yahoo! Finance article and posted by Smellfixers team

It actually already is, at least for past coronaviruses.
Generally, ozone has been proven to kill 99.999 percent of pathogens in the air, including SARS Coronavirus and influenzas such as H5N1, researchers anticipate that it may be an important tool in preventing the spread of the Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19).

What is optimal ozoning cycle?

Inspired by this Yahoo! Finance article and posted by Smellfixers team

We recommend - after the equipment producers - to start slowly, form a cycle of 10-30 minutes in a typical small room (of up to 10 sq. meters), then let the amassed ozone concentration work its way in on the surface and in the air for an hour.
Repeat the cycle a couple of times changing the location of the ozone generator so the ozone stream (all our equipment has 1-direction fans) works on all points of concern in the room.
The 10-30 mins should typically suffice in most situations and it also ensures no overly toxic cocnentration for humans will amass in the room space, at the same time being enough to destroy germs present in the air or on surfaces. This duration is also what most of our generators were designed to run for.

  1. Set first run to maximum 30 minutes - the smaller the room (below 5 sq. meters the less time you need, with minimum of 10 minutes.
  2. After the first run pre-programmed machine will stop and you should not enter the room for a minumum of another 30 mins, the bigger the room the more time you should leave for ozone to break down and decrease the toxicity level to tolerable.
  3. After that wait time you may enter the room to open windows and let the air in to further dissolve ozone.
    You should do this in a mask (and always in a mask if you suffer from asthma) - surgical or even cotton mask with a few layers should suffice. Once you have mask you can stay in the room longer, wipe and clean surfaces or hoover.
  4. After about 45-60 mins of ventilation you can reapeat the cycle again, form a different corener of the room if you wish or the are hidden space where you suspect ozon have not penetrated.
Usually one cycle per small room (of up to 50 cubic meters) should suffice.
